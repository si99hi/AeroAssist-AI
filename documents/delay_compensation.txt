United Airlines Delay Compensation Policy

Passengers delayed more than 3 hours due to circumstances within United's control may be eligible for
meal vouchers. Delays exceeding 6 hours may qualify for hotel accommodation when an overnight stay
is required.

United is not required to provide compensation for delays caused by weather, air traffic control,
or other extraordinary circumstances beyond the airline's control.

For significant delays, check the United app for updated gate information and rebooking options.
Elite MileagePlus members receive priority rebooking assistance during irregular operations.
