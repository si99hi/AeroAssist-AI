United Airlines Cancellation and Rebooking Policy

If your flight is cancelled by United Airlines, you may rebook on the next available flight at no
extra cost, or request a full refund to your original form of payment.

Rebooking after a voluntary cancellation may incur a change fee depending on your fare type.
Basic Economy tickets are generally non-refundable and non-changeable except as required by law.

For weather-related cancellations, United will automatically rebook passengers on the next available
flight. You may also choose to cancel and receive a travel credit valid for 12 months.

Passengers may manage rebooking through the United app, website, or airport service desk.
