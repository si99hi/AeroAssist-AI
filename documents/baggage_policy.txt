United Airlines Baggage Policy

Passengers may carry one personal item and one carry-on bag free of charge on all United flights.
Checked bags over 23kg (50 lbs) incur an overweight fee. Economy passengers may check up to two bags
for an additional fee per bag. First and business class passengers receive additional checked bag allowances.

Oversized items including sporting equipment may require special handling fees. Contact United customer
service before travel for items exceeding standard dimensions.
